This is a small yet incredibly charming and emotionally resonant Game Boy Color game called RAVEN-STAR. With its unique narrative and gameplay, it offers players a touching adventure about finding one's place in the world.​ In the game, players will take on the role of a little raven with an unfortunate fate. Born without wings, this flaw leads to its rejection by the flock, and it is mercilessly cast out of the village. However, the deep longing and yearning for home serve as a powerful driving force for it to embark on an adventurous journey. Players need to control this brave wingless raven, constantly running and nimbly jumping in the vast and mysterious landscape known as "The Ravine." On this challenging and unknown path, the goal is to seek out the legendary Wishing Star. Perhaps only by harnessing the magical power of the Wishing Star can the raven regain its wings and return to the home it dreams of.​ RAVEN-STAR is a meticulously crafted creation by developers using GB Studio. Whether you want to start this adventure immediately in your browser, download it as a ROM file to relive the classic handheld gaming experience on an emulator, or use the original Game Boy Color hardware to enjoy the authentic fun, the choice is yours. Come and join this adventure filled with warmth and challenges, and help the little raven find its rightful place!
