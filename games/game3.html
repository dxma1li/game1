PEREELOUS

SPIN YOUR WAY THROUGH THIS UNIQUE ROGUELIKE GAME

HOW TO PLAY ?

Use your mouse to interact with the game and send a wide variety of reels spinning.

Dive into a retro-inspired dungeon-crawling RPG where luck meets strategy in a one-armed bandit twist! SPIN YOUR WAY THROUGH THIS UNIQUE ROGUELIKE blends classic RPG elements with the thrill of randomized chaos, challenging you to battle bizarre monsters, hoard treasures, and navigate procedurally generated dungeons—all while spinning the reels of fate to shape your journey.

If you’re a fan of roguelikes like Slay the Spire or Enter the Gungeon, crave retro aesthetics, or just love the thrill of high-stakes, strategic gameplay, this title belongs in your library. It’s a love letter to classic dungeon crawlers, reimagined with the unpredictable soul of a roguelike.
